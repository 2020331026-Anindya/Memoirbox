# Code of Conduct

This project adheres to the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
We expect everyone who participates in the MemoirBox community to be respectful, inclusive, and considerate.

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at <maintainer@example.com>.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

For the full version of the Contributor Covenant, visit the link above.
